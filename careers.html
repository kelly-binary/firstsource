Why Iran?

Out of all the countries in the world, why did we decide to set up base in Iran? It’s simple. According to the World Economic Forum, Iran ranks high on the list of the top countries with the most engineering graduates, making it a major repository of IT talent. However, the talent pool is relatively untapped due to the lack of foreign investment in the country.

We aim to tap into Iran’s large pool of IT talent who we believe to have world-class potential. Come and join us in developing state-of-the-art financial software solutions that make an impact around the world.

Senior Front-End Developer

First Source Solutions is searching for talented and motivated Senior Front-End Developers who are looking for a chance to excel.

If you have the skillset we seek, you’ll become a valued member of highly competent front-end development team.

Duties and responsibilities

As our Senior Front-End Developer, you will be taking on the challenge of developing and maintaining advanced applications and interfaces, as well as testing and debugging complex technical and UI issues.

You will:

  • Develop and maintain advanced features tools, and applications according to best practices in UI/UX, front-end development, and hybrid mobile development.

  • Keep up with the latest Javascript frameworks, libraries, and tools for better web and mobile development

To apply, please submit your CV and cover letter to careers@firstsource.io

Senior Back-End Developer

First Source Solutions is seeking a driven, proactive, and highly-experienced Senior Back-End Developer. You will write robust, high-quality, and production-ready code that powers our websites and applications. Your work will directly influence the core architecture that drives our high-traffic binary options trading website.

You should be technically strong, and proficient in the following:

  • Experience with languages such as Perl, Python, PHP, C/C++, Go, or Ruby, and a willingness to become highly proficient with Perl.
  • Expertise in Linux system administration.
  • Experience with relational database design, and/or open-source RDBMS systems such as MySQL and PostgreSQL.
  • Familiarity with Perl DBI, Moose, PSGI/Plack, NGINX, JavaScript, Redis, and Git.
  • Ability to produce high-quality, self-documenting code by using test-driven development (TDD) techniques.
  • Passion for Linux and other open-source platforms.

Your role
First Source Solutions’s IT team is responsible for the design, development, and operation of our high-traffic networks. As our Senior Back-End Developer, you will be taking on the challenge of writing robust, high-quality, and production-ready code. Your work will greatly contribute to the architecture that drives our high-traffic binary options trading website.
